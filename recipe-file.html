Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Return

Preperation Time : 15 minutes Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta or guanciale until it becomes crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the crispy pancetta, then pour the egg and cheese mixture over it.
  5. Toss everything together until the pasta is coated in the creamy sauce. Add freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and black pepper.

Additional Info

Origin: Italy

Difficulty: 3 (Moderate)

Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Return

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add diced onions and minced garlic; cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the beef mixture. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Additional Info

Origin: Italy

Difficulty: 2 (Easy)

Rating: 4.8

Vegetarian Sushi Recipe

Return

Preparation Time: 15 minutes | Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Assorted vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado, carrot)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on the bamboo mat and wet your fingers to spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori.
  4. Add your choice of thinly sliced vegetables on top of the rice.
  5. Roll the nori and rice tightly using the bamboo mat.
  6. Slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger if desired.

Speedy Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

Return

Preparation Time: 10 minutes | Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Broccoli florets
  • Carrots, sliced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sliced chicken breast. Cook until the chicken is browned.
  3. Add broccoli florets and sliced carrots. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Drizzle with soy sauce and red pepper flakes (optional). Stir well.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your Speedy Chicken Stir-Fry!

Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe

Return

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken pieces (legs and thighs)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. In a large, shallow dish, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Remove chicken from buttermilk, allowing excess to drip off, and coat each piece in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or skillet to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through, about 15-20 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels, and serve your Crispy Fried Chicken hot and delicious!

Homemade Margherita Pizza Recipe

Return

Preparation Time: 15 minutes | Cooking Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 500°F or 260°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Place slices of fresh mozzarella cheese evenly on top of the sauce.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Transfer the pizza to a hot pizza stone or baking sheet and bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned, about 10-12 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy your Homemade Margherita Pizza!

Classic Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Return

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil and butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add chopped onions and garlic. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  4. Add mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and become tender.
  5. Sprinkle flour over the mushroom mixture and stir well.
  6. Slowly add beef broth while stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  7. Reduce heat to low and return the cooked beef to the skillet.
  8. Stir in sour cream and cook until heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve your Classic Beef Stroganoff over cooked noodles or rice.

Additional Info

Origin: Russia

Difficulty: 3 (Moderate)
