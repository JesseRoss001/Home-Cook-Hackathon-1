Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preperation Time : 15 minutes Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta or guanciale until it becomes crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the crispy pancetta, then pour the egg and cheese mixture over it.
  5. Toss everything together until the pasta is coated in the creamy sauce. Add freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and black pepper.

Additional Info

Origin: Italy

Difficulty: 3 (Moderate)

Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add diced onions and minced garlic; cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the beef mixture. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken pieces (legs and thighs)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. In a large, shallow dish, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Remove chicken from buttermilk, allowing excess to drip off, and coat each piece in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or skillet to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through, about 15-20 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels, and serve your Crispy Fried Chicken hot and delicious!

Classic Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil and butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add chopped onions and garlic. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  4. Add mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and become tender.
  5. Sprinkle flour over the mushroom mixture and stir well.
  6. Slowly add beef broth while stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.
  7. Reduce heat to low and return the cooked beef to the skillet.
  8. Stir in sour cream and cook until heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve your Classic Beef Stroganoff over cooked noodles or rice.

Additional Info

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 15 minutes | Cooking Time: 20 minutes

  • 8 oz linguine pasta
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  7. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and heat through.

Mushroom Risotto Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 10 minutes | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 8 oz mushrooms (e.g., cremini or shiitake), sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth, heated
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sliced mushrooms. Sauté until the mushrooms are tender and browned. Remove half of the mushrooms and set them aside.
  3. Add Arborio rice to the skillet and stir to coat the rice with oil.
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook until it's mostly absorbed by the rice.
  5. Begin adding the heated broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and allowing the liquid to be absorbed before adding more.
  6. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked to your desired level of doneness (about 18-20 minutes).
  7. Stir in the reserved sautéed mushrooms, grated Parmesan cheese, and butter. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve your Mushroom Risotto hot, garnished with chopped parsley.

Additional Info

Origin: Italy

Difficulty: 3 (Moderate)

Rating: 4.8

Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 10 minutes | Baking Time: 12-15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and salt until smooth.
  4. Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs until the mixture is creamy.
  5. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients and fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Enjoy your homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup bell peppers (red, green, and yellow), diced
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (canned or fresh)
  • 1/4 cup onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  3. In a separate pan, sauté the diced onion and bell peppers until they become tender.
  4. In a small bowl, mix ketchup, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, cornstarch, and water to make the sweet and sour sauce.
  5. Pour the sweet and sour sauce over the sautéed vegetables and bring to a boil. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add the fried chicken and pineapple chunks to the sauce. Stir to coat the chicken evenly.
  7. Serve hot over steamed rice and enjoy your homemade Sweet and Sour Chicken!

Tiramisu Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 30 minutes | Chilling Time: 4 hours

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Semi-sweet chocolate shavings, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a heatproof bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and whisk continuously until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the egg yolk mixture and whisk until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. In a shallow dish, combine brewed espresso or coffee with coffee liqueur (if using).
  5. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture briefly and arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers at the bottom of a serving dish.
  6. Add a layer of the mascarpone mixture on top of the ladyfingers. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of mascarpone mixture on top.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
  8. Before serving, dust the top with unsweetened cocoa powder and garnish with chocolate shavings.

Additional Info

Chicken Vindaloo Recipe

Difficulty Return Time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes | Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 4-6 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 6-8 garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon black mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak dried red chilies in hot water for 15-20 minutes. Drain and make a paste with ginger and garlic.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and black mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add the chili-ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few minutes.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  7. Add chicken pieces and sauté for a few minutes until they turn white.
  8. Stir in vinegar and garam masala. Mix well.
  9. Add some water, cover, and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  10. Serve your spicy Chicken Vindaloo with rice or naan. Enjoy!

Additional Info

Origin: India

Difficulty: 4.7 (Spicy)
